Our Work

Educational Content

Our website hosts a vast library of articles, guides, and videos that cover various aspects of dementia, ranging from early detection to effective caregiving techniques.

Webinars and Workshops

We organize webinars and workshops led by experts in the field, providing opportunities for interactive learning and skill development.

Caregiver Support

We offer dedicated support for caregivers through articles, webinars, and personalized assistance to help them cope with the challenges of dementia care.

Advocacy and Awareness

We actively advocate for dementia-friendly policies and raise awareness about the challenges faced by individuals living with dementia and their families.

Collaboration

Healthcare Professionals

Working hand-in-hand with healthcare experts allows us to create evidence-based content and provide comprehensive support to families and individuals affected by dementia.

Support Groups and Organizations

We partner with local and global support groups and organizations to expand our reach and offer diverse perspectives on dementia care.

Research Institutions

Collaborating with research institutions helps us stay at the forefront of advancements in dementia research and offer the most current information to our users.
